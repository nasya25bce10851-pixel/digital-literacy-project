Cybercrime Prevention Checklist

1.Use strong and unique passwords for all your online accounts.
2.Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) whenever it is available.
3.Avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading unknown email attachments.
4.Keep your devices, software, and antivirus programs updated regularly.
5.Do not share personal or financial information on untrusted websites.
6.Check if a website is secure (look for HTTPS) before entering sensitive information.
7.Be careful when using public Wi-Fi for online transactions.
8.Regularly back up important data to avoid losing it during cyber attacks.
9.Monitor your accounts for any unusual or suspicious activity.
10.Stay informed about common cyber threats such as phishing and online scams
