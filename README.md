## Project Overview
This repository contains my **Digital Literacy Project** completed as part of the CSE0001 course. The purpose of this project is to understand how digital tools and online platforms can be used responsibly, safely, and professionally.

Through a series of practical tasks, the project explores different aspects of digital literacy including creating digital content, building a professional online presence, practicing safe internet habits, communicating professionally through email, and understanding cybersecurity risks.

Each task folder in this repository contains the required files, screenshots, activity proofs, and brief notes related to that task.

---

## Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic
In this task, I designed an infographic explaining the concept of **digital literacy** and its importance in today's digital world. The infographic highlights key topics such as safe internet practices, maintaining a professional online profile, and proper email etiquette.

## Task 2 – Professional Online Portfolio
In this task, I created and updated profiles on professional digital platforms to build a basic online portfolio.

Platforms included:

- GitHub – used for hosting projects and managing code repositories  
- LinkedIn – used to create a professional profile and connect with professionals  
- Kaggle – used for exploring datasets and learning data science concepts  

Screenshots of the profiles along with a short reflection about creating a professional online presence are included in the corresponding folder.

---

## Task 3 – Digital Platforms Activity
This task involved exploring digital platforms and completing small activities to understand how online tools support learning and collaboration.

Screenshots of the activities, platform usage, and brief notes explaining the tasks performed are included in the folder as proof of completion.


---

## Task 4 – Email Etiquette and Responsible Online Behaviour
This task focuses on professional digital communication. I created sample email drafts that follow proper email etiquette such as clear subject lines, polite greetings, concise communication, and proper sign-offs.

In addition, a checklist was prepared describing responsible and respectful behavior on social media platforms.

Both the email drafts and the checklist are included in the task folder.

---

## Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
This task focuses on understanding cybercrime and the importance of cybersecurity. A cybercrime case study was researched and analyzed to understand the nature of the incident, its impact, and the lessons that can be learned from it.

A short prevention checklist was also created to highlight important practices individuals can follow to stay safe online.

The folder contains the case study and the prevention checklist.

Through this project, I learned:

- The importance of **digital literacy in modern life**
- How to build a **professional online presence**
- The importance of **safe internet practices**
- How to communicate professionally through **email etiquette**
- Awareness of **cybersecurity risks and prevention methods**
