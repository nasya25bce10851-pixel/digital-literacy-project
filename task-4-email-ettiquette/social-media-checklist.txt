STAY SAFE ONLINE-CHECKLIST
Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or banking details with anyone
Do not click on suspicious links or unknown messages
Always verify payment requests before approving them on UPI apps
Remember that receiving money does not require entering your UPI PIN
Use strong passwords and enable screen lock on your phone
Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
Regularly check your bank statements and transaction history
Install apps only from trusted sources like the Play Store or App Store
💳 UPI Safety Tips
Double-check the receiver’s name before making any payment
Do not approve “collect requests” unless you are sure about the sender
🚨 Reporting Cybercrime
Report incidents at: https://www.cybercrime.gov.in
Call helpline: 1930 (24x7)
